GO WON

Go Won (Hangul: 고원) is the eleventh revealed member of LOONA and a member of its third sub-unit, LOONA / yyxy . Go Won is the eleventh revealed member of LOONA and a member of its third sub-unit, LOONA / yyxy .
She was born as Park Chae-Won (Hangul: 박채원) on November 19, 2000, in Jung District, Incheon, South Korea.
She debuted on January 15, 2018 as a member and subsequently released her solo single album Go Won on January 30, 2018.
